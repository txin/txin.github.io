Hello! I am Tianxin Tang, a postdoctoral researcher at Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e), hosted by Andreas Hülsing in the Applied and Provable Security Group. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zurich in the Applied Cryptography Group led by Kenny Paterson. I received my Ph.D. under the supervision of Sasha Boldyreva from the Georgia Institute of Technology, USA.
